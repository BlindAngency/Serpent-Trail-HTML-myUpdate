The Serpent Trail Is an inter-denominational ministry dedicated to discerning by the Word of God the devil's devices/tricks in this end-time, because overcoming is recognizing the enemy in all his tactics. Our focus is on drawing young believers closer to Christ because we know through fellowship in a breaking of bread (organizing Christian gathering) and prayer, encouraging one another to live according to God's Word. I have witness that as a young believer since the time that I have given my life to Christ at the crossroads of my life on my teen years and later I have been a are victim of circumstances. Do you realize, old men, when we were boys, our boys have gotten times the temptation that we had? Sister, do you realize that your daughter has ten times the temptation you had when you were a girl? What will her daughter have? Look at the things, the pictures the devil is a painting. This organization is a non-profit organization dedicated to the cause of Jesus Christ alone, not affiliated with any denomination and is not supported by any governing church body. We do not subscribe to any man-made creeds and our only “doctrinal statement” is our belief in the absolute authority of the Word of God. We acknowledge only the headship of the Lord Jesus Christ, which was manifested again in this end-time.

Fellowship in the Breaking of Bread:

We gather regularly for activities like youth events, field gatherings, visits, and family activities, where we share food together. These gatherings are an expression of our shared faith and provide an opportunity to strengthen the ties that bind us in Christ’s love in a warm and welcoming environment.

Worship:

Our worship is centered on heartfelt, sincere expressions of praise to God. We move beyond polished performances and intellectual displays of music, focusing on Spirit-led worship that comes from the heart. It’s about glorifying God in truth and simplicity, not perfecting songs or voices.

Biblical Engagement Activities:

The Bible is at the center of everything we do. We encourage all believers to engage deeply with the Scriptures, learning from Christ through the teachings of the Prophet and applying them to their daily lives.
